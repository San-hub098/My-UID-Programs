About Amaravati Campus

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Amaravati Campus is located in Andhra Pradesh. It offers quality education with modern infrastructure and advanced laboratories. The campus provides a peaceful environment that supports learning and research. It focuses on academic excellence, innovation, and overall student development.

About Amritapuri Campus

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Amritapuri Campus is located in Kerala near the Arabian Sea. It is the headquarters of the university and offers a peaceful learning environment. The campus provides courses in engineering, arts, science, and social work. It focuses on academic excellence along with spiritual and value-based education.

About Bengaluru Campus

The Bengaluru Campus is known for its strong focus on engineering and technology programs. It offers modern laboratories and research facilities. The campus encourages innovation and industry collaboration. It provides students with excellent academic and placement opportunities.

About Chennai Campus

The Chennai Campus specializes mainly in engineering education. It provides advanced infrastructure and technical training. The campus promotes research and practical learning. It prepares students for successful careers in technology fields.

About Coimbatore Campus

The Coimbatore Campus is one of the largest campuses of the university. It offers diverse programs in engineering, management, arts, and science. The campus has excellent research centers and modern facilities. It focuses on academic excellence and holistic student development.

About Faridabad Campus

The Faridabad Campus is located near New Delhi. It offers programs in engineering, management, and medical sciences. The campus emphasizes research and innovation. It provides strong industry exposure and career support.

About Kochi Campus

The Kochi Campus is well known for medical and health science programs. It includes a teaching hospital with advanced healthcare facilities. The campus provides quality education in medicine and nursing. It focuses on service-oriented and value-based learning.

About Mysuru Campus

The Mysuru Campus offers programs in engineering and postgraduate studies. It provides a calm and focused learning environment. The campus supports research and skill development. It encourages students to achieve academic and professional excellence.

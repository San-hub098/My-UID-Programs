Amrita Vishwavidyapeetham

Mata Amritanandamayi Devi(born Sudhamani Idamannel on September 27,1953),

known fondly as Amma("Mother")or the "hugging saint,"is a world-renowned

Indian spiritual leader and humanitarian from Kerala.She is globally recognized

for embracing millions as a profound act of compassion,offering unconditional

love to alleviate physical and emotional suffering.
